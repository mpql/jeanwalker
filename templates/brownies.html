LJ Brownies

This is a quick and delicious recipe for brownies that Jean would whip up for a quick treat for Bridge Club, to take to a friend on the mend or for a visitor. She didn't enjoy cooking, but these brownies were a quick crowd pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 1 stick butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup flour
  • ½ cup cocoa powder

Optional Additions

  • Peanut butter swirled into the top
  • Chocolate Chips (PB chips or butterscotch chips)
  • Nuts of your choice

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 300 degrees.
  2. Butter and flour a 8x8 pan.
  3. In a large bowl or mixer: cream butter and sugar together.
  4. Add eggs and mix.
  5. Mix in flour and cocoa powder.
  6. Pour into baking dish
  7. Bake for 20-25 mins—they are ready when a knife comes out clean.
